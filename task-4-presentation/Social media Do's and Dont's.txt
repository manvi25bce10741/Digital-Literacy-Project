SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS


DO'S:
1. Maintain a professional profile
2. Always think before you post
3. Protect your personal information
4. Use privacy settings
5. Be respectful in interactions


DON'TS:
1. Do not share sensitive information
2. Avoid posting inappropriate content
3. Do not engage in online arguments
4. Do not click on unknown links
5. Do not spread misinformation
