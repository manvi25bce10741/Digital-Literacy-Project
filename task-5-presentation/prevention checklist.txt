STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST


1. Do not click on suspicious links or emails
2. Use strong and unique passwords
3. Enable two-factor authentication
4. Always verify the sender before sharing information
5. Do not share OTP or banking details with anyone
6. Regularly update your passwords
7. Always check website URLs before logging in
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions


Financial/UPI Safety:
9. Never share UPI PIN with anyone
10. Verify payment requests before accepting


Reporting:
Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in
