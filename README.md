Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The aim of the project was to understand how digital tools and platforms can be used effectively in academic and everyday situations. The project follows the role of a “Student Digital Ambassador,” where different tasks were performed to explore areas like digital awareness, online presence, communication, and cyber safety.
Each task focuses on a different aspect of digital literacy and involves practical work such as creating content, setting up profiles, and using online tools.
Module 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness
In this module, I created an infographic using Canva to explain what digital literacy is and why it is important. The infographic also includes useful tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. This task helped me understand how information can be presented visually in a simple and effective way.
Module 2 – Digital Portfolio
In this module, I set up and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional online presence. This task helped me understand how these profiles can be useful for internships and future opportunities.
Module 3 – Platforms and Collaboration
In this module, I explored a coding platform by solving a beginner-level problem on HackerRank. I also created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy and generated a response sheet. This helped me learn how these tools can be used for practice and collaboration.
Module 4 – Email Etiquette
In this module, I wrote two professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media use. This task helped me understand how to communicate properly in formal situations and the importance of maintaining professionalism online.
Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this module, I studied identity theft as a form of cybercrime and created a case study along with a prevention checklist. This helped me understand how such crimes happen and how they can be avoided through awareness and careful online behavior.
